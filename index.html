About Us

Data Berlin Meetup is Berlin's vibrant community of data professionals, enthusiasts, and learners, bringing together over 1,800 members passionate about data.

What We Do

We organize regular meetups, workshops, and networking events focused on data-related technologies and techniques. Our events cover a wide range of topics including:

  • Data Analytics & Business Intelligence
  • Data Engineering & DataOps
  • Data Visualization
  • Machine Learning & AI
  • Big Data Technologies

Our Community

Join our growing community where you can:

  • Network with data professionals from Berlin's tech scene
  • Share knowledge and experiences
  • Find collaboration opportunities
  • Stay updated with the latest data trends
